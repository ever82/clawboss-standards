# Life

> Daily life and health management.

## Rules

1. **Regular Schedule**: Maintain regular sleep and diet
2. **Moderate Exercise**: Exercise at least 3 times per week
3. **Mindfulness**: Enjoy life, don't always be busy
4. **Relationships**: Maintain important relationships
5. **Self-Care**: Take care of your physical and mental health

## Experiences

- Early to bed and early to rise is much more efficient than staying up late
- The 2 hours after exercise is the golden time for productive work
- 10 minutes of meditation can significantly reduce anxiety
- Regularly disconnect and rest, stay away from information overload
- Gratitude journaling can increase happiness

## Methods

### Morning Routine

Start an efficient day.

1. Wake up early (6-7 AM)
2. Drink a glass of warm water
3. Light exercise or stretching
4. Meditate for 5-10 minutes
5. Plan today's 3 important things

### Evening Routine

Ensure sleep quality.

1. Stay away from screens 1 hour before bed
2. Organize tomorrow's task list
3. Write gratitude journal
4. Read paper books
5. Sleep before 11 PM
