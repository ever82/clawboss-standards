# Reading

> Acquire knowledge through reading books, articles, documentation, etc.

## Rules

1. **Pre-read Screening**: Read table of contents and summary first, judge value before diving deep
2. **Active Reading**: Read with questions and purpose
3. **Mark and Annotate**: Mark important content, write down thoughts and questions
4. **Control Speed**: Adjust reading speed based on content difficulty
5. **Output Notes**: Must output structured notes after reading

## Experiences

- Technical books: Skim once to build framework, then intensive read key chapters
- Non-fiction: Focus on arguments, evidence, and conclusions
- Fiction: Enjoy the reading process, don't force speed
- Use Zotero or Notion to manage reading lists
- Read 3-5 books simultaneously, switch based on mood to maintain interest

## Methods

### Progressive Reading

Applicable for long articles and technical documentation.

1. **First pass**: Quick skim, grasp main idea (5-10 minutes)
2. **Second pass**: Read key points carefully, understand logic
3. **Third pass**: Deep research, look up references

### Topic Reading Method

Quickly master a new domain.

1. Select a topic, collect 5-10 related books/articles
2. Skim each one, extract core viewpoints
3. Compare different authors' perspectives, form your own understanding
4. Output a review article
