# Writing

> Organize thinking and express ideas through writing.

## Rules

1. **Write Daily**: Maintain daily writing habit, quantity over quality
2. **Clear Structure**: Article structure should be clear and logical
3. **Audience First**: Consider reader needs and perspective
4. **Iterate and Polish**: Good writing comes from repeated revision
5. **Publish and Share**: Share outputs to get feedback

## Experiences

- Free writing for 10 minutes can overcome writer's block
- Morning is golden time for writing, brain is clearest
- Reading extensively helps improve writing skills
- Good titles increase click-through rates by 50%
- Writing notes while reading improves retention

## Methods

### Free Writing

Write continuously without stopping.

1. Set a 10-minute timer
2. Write non-stop, don't worry about grammar or logic
3. Don't delete or modify
4. Quantity first, quality second
5. After finishing, select valuable content to polish

### SCQA Model

Structured writing framework.

- **S**ituation: Describe background and current situation
- **C**omplication: Point out conflict or problem
- **Q**uestion: Pose key question
- **A**nswer: Provide solution or answer

### Feynman Writing

Explain complex concepts in simple language.

1. Choose a complex concept
2. Write an article explaining it to a layman
3. Identify parts where explanation is unclear
4. Go back and study unclear parts
5. Rewrite using analogies and examples
